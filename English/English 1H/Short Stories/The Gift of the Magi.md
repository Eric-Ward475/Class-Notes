- Della and Jim are a married couple who are completely broke but want to buy each other meaningful Christmas gifts.
- Della’s most valued possession is her long, beautiful hair; Jim’s is his heirloom gold watch.
- Della cuts and sells her hair to buy Jim a chain for his watch.
- Jim sells his watch to buy Della expensive combs for her hair.
- They both end up with gifts they can’t use, but their sacrifices show how much they truly love each other.
