**Summary**
- Ulrich von Gradwitz and Georg Znaeym are bitter enemies feuding over a strip of forest land.
- One stormy night, both men patrol the forest hoping to confront the other
- They meet, but before either can act, a tree falls and traps them both under its branches
- At first, they curse and threaten each other, still clinging to their hatred
- Over time, as they lie there helpless, Ulrich offers peace and friendship, Georg initially disagrees, but eventually gives in
- They decide to end their feud and think of how shocked their men will be when they see them getting along with eachother
