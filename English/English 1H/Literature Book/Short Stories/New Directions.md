**Summary**
- Written by Maya Angelou
- Tells the story of Annie Johnson, a black woman living in the early 1900s
- Annie's husband left her for another woman
- Having no money, and small children to raise, Annie started a business selling food to workers
- She worked hard, carrying food the to 2 different worksites, until she was sure that the workers relied on her for food
- She then set up a more permanent shop between the 2 sites

**Theme**
- Positive attitude
- Consistency rewards in the long run
- Good work ethic is important
