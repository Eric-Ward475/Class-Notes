### Eukaryotic
###### Characteristics
- Has a nucleus
- Membrane bound organelles
- More complex

---
### Prokaryotic
###### Characteristics
- No nucleus
	- DNA is free floating in the cytoplasm of the cell, in a region called the nucleotide
- No membrane bound organelles
- Always has a cell wall
- Contains ribosomes

---
### Comparison
- Eukaryotic are more complex, favored in multicellular life
- Prokaryotes include bacteria, archaea and protists
