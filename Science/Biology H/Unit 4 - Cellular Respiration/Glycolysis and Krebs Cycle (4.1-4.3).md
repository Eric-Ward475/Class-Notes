
### Equation of Cellular Respiration

$C_{2}H_{12}O_{6} + 6CO_{2}$ ---> $6CO_{2} + 6H_{2}O + ATP$

**Conservation of Matter**
- No atoms are destroyed or created in the reaction
### Glycolysis

- **Reactants**: Glucose; ATP; ADP
- **Products**: NADH; ATP; Pyruvate
- **Location**: Cytoplasm
- **Purpose**: Break glucose into two pyruvate
- Anaerobic
